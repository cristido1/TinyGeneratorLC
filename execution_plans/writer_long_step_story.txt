GENERAL RULES
	•	Memory keys can store up to 15,000 characters.
	•	Long memories are allowed. Do not refuse.
	•	Always follow the steps exactly.
	•	Continue the algorithm until all 6 chapters are created.
	•	Do not skip steps.
	•	Do not shorten the story unless explicitly told.

⸻

WORKFLOW (MUST FOLLOW ALL STEPS)

Step 1 — Create the Full Plot

Create a complex sci-fi plot divided into 6 chapters.
Describe what happens in each chapter.
Use up to 13,000 characters.
Save to memory: storia1_trama.

⸻

Step 2 — Create the Character List

List all main characters with:
	•	name
	•	gender
	•	approximate age
	•	personality traits
Save to memory: storia1_personaggi.

⸻

Step 3 — Write Chapter 1

Write Chapter 1 using up to 13,000 characters, including:
	•	narration
	•	dialogues
	•	environmental descriptions
Save to memory: storia1_capitolo1.

⸻

Step 4 — Summary of Chapter 1

Summarize the events of Chapter 1.
Save to: storia1_riassunto1.

⸻

Step 5 — Write Chapter 2

Read:
	•	storia1_trama
	•	storia1_personaggi
	•	storia1_riassunto1
Write Chapter 2 (up to 13,000 characters).
Save to: storia1_capitolo2.

⸻

Step 6 — Summary of Chapter 2

Summarize the events of Chapter 2.
Save to: storia1_riassunto2.

⸻

Step 7 — Combined Summary for Chapter 3

Combine storia1_riassunto1 and storia1_riassunto2.
Save to: storia1_riassunto_totale2.

⸻

Step 8 — Write Chapter 3

Read:
	•	storia1_trama
	•	storia1_personaggi
	•	storia1_riassunto_totale2
Write Chapter 3 (up to 13,000 characters).
Save to: storia1_capitolo3.

⸻

Step 9 — Summary of Chapter 3

Summarize Chapter 3.
Save to: storia1_riassunto3.

⸻

Step 10 — Continue the Same Pattern for Chapters 4, 5, 6

For Chapter n = 4, 5, 6:
	1.	Combine all previous chapter summaries.
Save to: storia1_riassunto_totale(n-1)
	2.	Write Chapter n (up to 13,000 characters) using:
	•	trama
	•	personaggi
	•	combined summary
Save to: storia1_capitolo(n)
	3.	Summarize Chapter n.
Save to: storia1_riassunto(n)

Continue until all 6 chapters are complete.

⸻

END CONDITION

Stop only when:
	•	Chapters 1–6 are written
	•	Summaries 1–6 are saved
	•	Combined summaries are saved correctly
